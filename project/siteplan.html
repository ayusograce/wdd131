Site Plan Project

Site Name

Irresistible Gourmet

This name a bakery that makes delicious cakes and baked goods. This is the actual name of the bakery.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about the products that are made in the bakery. It also provides contact information to purchase them.

Scenarios

What are the products being sold at the bakery? How can I contact the bakery to buy their products? What does the products look like?

Color Schema

F21534: the color of the buttons

A52A2A: the color of the background of the footer and the menu. Also the color of the headings.

FFFFFF: the color of the whole page and the text where the background is A52A2A

Typography

Raleway: titles of section and letter that are big

Roboto: for the paragraphs. Mainly in the body, where information needed to be provided.
